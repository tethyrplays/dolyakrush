Explore Tyria with Friends

Dolyak Rush [DLYK] is a group of very friendly and helpful players that frequently team up to master the challenges of PvE content and achievements in Guild Wars 2.

Each week we host Guild Missions, run Tier 4 Fractal dailies, provide help for newer players, and more. Members are also provided access to our Discord server to socialize and coordinate activities.
