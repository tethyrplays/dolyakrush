Explore Tyria with Friends

Dolyak Rush [DLYK] is a tight-knit group of very friendly and helpful players from across North America that frequently work together on open world PvE content and achievements in Guild Wars 2.

Each week we host Guild Missions, run Fractal dailies, training for newer players, work together through harder content.

Members are also provided access to our Discord server to socialize and coordinate activities.
