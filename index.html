Explore Tyria with Friends

Dolyak Rush [DLYK] is a very friendly, casual and helpful group of players that frequently team up to master the challenges of the open world and achievements in Guild Wars 2.

Each week we host Guild Missions, run Tier 4 Fractal dailies and provide help to newer players. Members also have access to our Discord server to socialize and coordinate activities.

Guild representation is optional, except during missions for reward credit.

GETTING STARTED

Visit the official Guild Wars 2 website to create an account and/or download the game client. Go now »

To participate in World vs World (WvW) with Dolyak Rush, select North America/Dragonbrand as the home server during character creation.

JOIN THE GUILD

We actively recruit players throughout the year. Send an in-game message or whisper (/w) one of our core members to request an invitation.
